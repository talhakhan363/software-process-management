1. PRE-DEVELOPMENT STAGE (GENERALIZED)
===========================================================================
The Pre-Development stage is critical for aligning technical feasibility 
with business goals before a single line of code is written.

Phase 1: Problem Discovering
- Identifying the gap between current state and desired state.
- Conducting stakeholder interviews and observation to find "pain points."

Phase 2: Risk Analysis
- Identifying technical, financial, and operational uncertainties.
- Assessing the probability of failure and planning mitigations.

Phase 3: Development Life Cycle (SDLC) Selection
- Choosing a model (Waterfall, Agile, Scrum) based on project clarity.
- Defining the roadmap and major milestones.

Phase 4: Support & Feasibility
- Determining long-term maintenance requirements.
- Ensuring resources (human and hardware) are available for the project.

===========================================================================
2. WHY A LEARNING MANAGEMENT SYSTEM (LMS)?
===========================================================================
An LMS was selected as the solution during the Problem Discovering stage 
due to the following needs:
- Centralized Resource Management: To prevent data loss and fragmentation.
- Automated Tracking: To monitor student progress and grading efficiently.
- Accessibility: Providing 24/7 access to educational materials remotely.
- Scalability: Handling large numbers of users without manual intervention.
